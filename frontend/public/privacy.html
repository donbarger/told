Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

IMB Innovation operates Told, an AI-powered interactive parables experience. Players step inside one of eleven parables Jesus told and make choices that shape the texture of the story. This policy describes what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you create an account or use the game:

  • Account information — your name and email address as provided by Google when you sign in
  • Game data — which stories you've played, the choices you made, your in-game stats, and your journal entries, stored so you can pick up where you left off
  • Usage data — basic server logs including IP addresses and request timestamps, retained for security and debugging purposes

We do not collect payment information, health data, or sensitive personal information. We do not track you across other websites.

2. How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate your account and maintain your session
  • Save and restore your game progress
  • Generate AI-powered narrative content
  • Monitor service health and investigate security issues

3. AI-Generated Content and Third-Party Services

Told uses a third-party AI provider to generate scenes between the fixed story beats. When you play, the current scene context — the story you've selected, the phase you're in, and your recent in-game choices — is sent to that provider so the next scene can be generated.

Game inputs are processed by our AI provider under its own privacy policy. Told does not prompt you to enter free-form text during gameplay, but please avoid entering sensitive personal information anywhere in the interface.

4. Google Sign-In

Told uses Google Sign-In as the sole authentication method. We receive your name, email address, and a unique Google account identifier. We use this only to create and authenticate your account. We do not access your Google contacts, calendar, Gmail, or any other Google services.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • AI provider — receives game context data (not your account info) to generate narrative content
  • Google — if you use Google Sign-In, authentication is handled by Google's OAuth service
  • Legal requirements — we may disclose data if required by law or to protect the safety of users

6. Data Retention

Your account and game saves are retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us (see Section 10). Deleted accounts are removed from our systems within 30 days.

Server logs are retained for up to 90 days for security purposes.

7. Security

Told has no passwords to store — authentication is handled entirely by Google OAuth. We use JWT tokens for session management and standard security practices to protect your data, but no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

Told is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data

To exercise these rights, contact us through imb.org.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of Told after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy should be directed through imb.org. Told is a tool of the IMB Innovation team.
