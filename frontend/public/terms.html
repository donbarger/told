2. Description of Service

Told is an AI-powered interactive parables experience. Players step inside one of eleven parables Jesus told — including the Prodigal Son, the Good Samaritan, Zacchaeus, the Rich Young Ruler, the Widow's Two Coins, and others — and make choices that shape the texture of the story. Each scene is a card; fixed story beats are written by hand, and scenes between them are generated in real time by AI.

Told is provided for devotional, educational, and discipleship purposes. It is not a replacement for Scripture itself, preaching, theological instruction, or pastoral counsel.

Told is a tool for reflection on the stories Jesus told — not a retelling that should displace Scripture. All AI-generated scenes are creative extensions of fixed story beats and do not represent the views of IMB personnel or the church.
